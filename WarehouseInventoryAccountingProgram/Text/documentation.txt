Warehouse Inventory Accounting Program Documentation

Welcome to the Warehouse Inventory Accounting Program!

This program is designed to help grocery stores manage their inventory.

Features:
- Inventory Management: Add, edit, and delete products from the inventory.
- Sales Processing: Record sales transactions and update inventory accordingly.
- Purchase Orders: Create purchase orders to restock inventory.
- Reports: Generate reports to analyze inventory, sales, and purchases.

Getting Started:
1. Log in using your credentials.
2. Use the navigation menu to access different forms and features.
3. Make sure to keep track of sales and restock products as needed.
4. Utilize the reports for data analysis and decision-making.

Feel free to explore and enjoy the program!
