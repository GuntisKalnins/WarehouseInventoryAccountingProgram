Troubleshooting and Common Issues

1. Unable to log in:
   - Make sure you are using the correct username and password.
   - Check your internet connection to ensure the program can connect to the database.

2. Inventory not updating after sales:
   - Verify that you have processed the sales correctly and the quantity sold is accurate.
   - Check for any error messages that might indicate database connectivity issues.

3. Purchase order not showing in the Purchase form:
   - Ensure the purchase order has a status of "Pending."
   - Check if the purchase order was created with the correct supplier and product information.

4. Reports not generating:
   - Double-check that you have selected the correct report option from the Reports form.
   - Verify that the data required for the report is available in the database.

If you encounter any other issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
